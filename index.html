Daniel 'MaTachi' Jonsson

Podcast junkie is also a fitting epithet. A small selection of some podcasts I enjoy listening to are Coder Radio, Linux Action Show, TechSNAP, Plan B, Let's Talk Bitcoin, TuxRadar, Off The Hook and Ubuntu Podcast.
